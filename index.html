How It Works

Landmarks

Identify BA, A1_L, A1_R, M1_L, M1_R on TOF and SNAP MRAs.

Affine Model

Compute translation, rotation, scaling, and shear.

Transformation

Apply transformation to full TOF centerline.

Output

Generate SNAP-aligned centerline for visualization and analysis.

Research Context

Multicontrast MRA enables comprehensive cerebrovascular analysis but requires accurate arterial centerlines across contrasts. TOF offers high spatial resolution, while SNAP improves vessel wall visualization but often omits distal branches.

Motivation

Manual centerline reconstruction exceeds three hours per scan.

Results

Automated conversion in under five minutes with preserved topology.

Impact

Enables visualization of arteries frequently omitted in SNAP images.
